Eco Warriors is a student-led organization dedicated to environmental advocacy and sustainable community action. We organize coastal clean-ups, tree planting drives, and recycling campaigns in partnership with barangay youth groups and campus offices. Our mission is to connect students with meaningful volunteer opportunities while promoting efficient use of shared resources across organizations.
