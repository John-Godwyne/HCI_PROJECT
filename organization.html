About

Eco Warriors is a student-led organization dedicated to environmental advocacy and sustainable community action. We organize coastal clean-ups, tree planting drives, and recycling campaigns in partnership with barangay youth groups and campus offices across Metro Manila.

Our Mission

To empower students and communities to take meaningful action for the environment through education, volunteerism, and sustainable practices that create lasting positive change.
